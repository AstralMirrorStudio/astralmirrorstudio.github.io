Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Built for quiet discipline. No social pressure, no leaderboards. Just you and your practice.

⚔️
27 Preset Habits
Choose from Body, Mind, Learning, Creative, and Life categories. Or create your own custom habits.
🥋
Belt Rank System
Earn XP with every habit. Rise through 10 belt ranks, each unlocking new features and wisdom.
🔥
Streak Multipliers
The longer you train, the more XP you earn. 7 days = 1.5x, 30 days = 2x. Consistency is rewarded.
✍️
Evening Journal
Reflect on each day with a single line. Your journal history is saved and reviewable anytime.
📊
Monthly Calendar
Visualize your consistency with a beautiful calendar showing completed days at a glance.
🌏
Japanese & English
Full support for both languages. Switch instantly from anywhere in the app.
